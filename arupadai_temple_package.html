Arupadai Murugan Temples

The Arupadai Murugan temples are six revered shrines in Tamil Nadu dedicated to Lord Murugan. They include Palani, Thiruthani, Tirupparankundram, Swamimalai, Karisal, and Azhagar Kovil, each known for its unique historical and spiritual significance. These temples offer devotees a deep spiritual experience and connection with the deity.
