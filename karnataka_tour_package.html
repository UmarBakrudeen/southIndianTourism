Karnataka: IT Capital of India

Karnataka, in southwestern India, is known for its rich culture, diverse landscapes, and vibrant cities like Bangalore. The state features historic sites, lush hills, wildlife, and scenic beaches, blending ancient traditions with modern development.

Package Detail

No. of Days: 15 Days
Day 1
Trichy to Mysore

Departure from Trichy: Early morning drive to Mysore (7-8 hours, ~400 km).

Enroute Stops: Karur, Dindigul, and Nanjangud for brief breaks.

Arrival in Mysore: Late afternoon.

Visit:
* Mysore Palace: Explore the grand palace of the Wodeyars.

Stay: Mysore

Day 2
Mysore Sightseeing

Visit:
* Chamundi Hill: Visit Chamundeshwari Temple.
* St. Philomena’s Church: A Gothic-style church.
* Brindavan Gardens: Famous for its musical fountains.

Stay: Mysore

Day 3
Mysore to Coorg

Travel to Coorg: Morning drive (3 hours, ~120 km).

Visit:
* Namdroling Monastery (Golden Temple): A Tibetan Buddhist temple.
* Abbey Falls: A picturesque waterfall.
* Raja’s Seat: A viewpoint with panoramic views.

Stay: Coorg

Day 4
Coorg Sightseeing

Visit:
* Talakaveri: The origin of the river Kaveri.
* Bhagamandala: A temple complex at the confluence of three rivers.
* Dubare Elephant Camp: Experience interacting with elephants.

Stay: Coorg

Day 5
Coorg to Hassan

Travel to Hassan: Morning drive (3.5 hours, ~130 km).

Visit:
* Belur: Chennakesava Temple, known for its intricate architecture.
* Halebidu: Hoysaleswara Temple, famous for its carvings.

Stay: Hassan

Day 6
Hassan to Chikmagalur

Travel to Chikmagalur: Morning drive (1.5 hours, ~60 km).

Visit:
* Mullayanagiri Peak: The highest peak in Karnataka.
* Baba Budangiri: A mountain range with scenic views.

Afternoon: Explore coffee plantations.

Stay: Chikmagalur

Day 7
Chikmagalur to Hampi

Travel to Hampi: Early morning drive (7 hours, ~340 km).

Evening: Relax and explore the local area around Hampi.

Stay: Hampi

Day 8
Hampi Sightseeing

Visit:
* Vijaya Vittala Temple: Known for its stone chariot and musical pillars.
* Virupaksha Temple: A historic temple still in use.
* Lotus Mahal & Elephant Stables: Examples of Indo-Islamic architecture.

Stay: Hampi

Day 9
Hampi to Murudeshwar

Travel to Murudeshwar: Early morning drive (8 hours, ~370 km).

Evening: Relax by the beach.

Stay: Murudeshwar

Day 10
Murudeshwar to Gokarna

Visit in Murudeshwar:
* Murudeshwar Temple: Home to the second tallest Shiva statue in the world.

Travel to Gokarna: Late morning drive (2 hours, ~80 km).

Visit:
* Mahabaleshwar Temple
* Om Beach

Stay: Gokarna

Day 11
Gokarna to Kollur

Travel to Kollur: Morning drive (3 hours, ~130 km).

Visit:
* Mookambika Temple: A revered pilgrimage site.

Evening: Explore the local area.

Stay: Kollur

Day 12
Kollur to Udupi

Travel to Udupi: Morning drive (2 hours, ~75 km).

Visit:
* Sri Krishna Temple
* Malpe Beach: Optional visit to St. Mary’s Island.

Stay: Udupi

Day 13
Udupi to Mangalore

Travel to Mangalore: Morning drive (1.5 hours, ~60 km).

Visit:
* Kadri Manjunath Temple
* Mangaladevi Temple
* St. Aloysius Chapel

Evening: Relax at Panambur Beach.

Stay: Mangalore

Day 14
Mangalore to Bangalore

Travel to Bangalore: Morning drive (7 hours, ~350 km).

Visit(if time permits):
* Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace


Evening: Free time for shopping or relaxation.

Stay: Bangalore

Day 15
Bangalore to Trichy

Return to Trichy: Early morning drive (6 hours, ~335 km).

Arrival in Trichy: Afternoon/evening.

Departure: Drop at Trichy airport/railway station.
