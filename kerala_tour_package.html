Kerala: God's Own Country

Kerala, known as "God's Own Country," is a beautiful state in South India famous for its lush landscapes, serene backwaters, and rich cultural heritage. Highlights include houseboat cruises in Alleppey, tea plantations in Munnar, and vibrant festivals like Onam. Kerala's unique blend of natural beauty, traditional charm, and flavorful cuisine makes it a top travel destination.

Package Detail

No. of Days: 10 Days
Day 1
Trichy to Thekkady

Departure from Trichy: Early morning drive to Thekkady (5.5 hours, ~270 km).

Afternoon: Check-in and relax.

Visit:
* Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary for a boat ride on Periyar Lake.
* Kathakali or Kalaripayattu show in the evening.

Stay: Thekkady

Day 2
Thekkady to Munnar

Morning: Visit a **spice plantation* in Thekkady.

Travel to Munnar: Late morning drive (3.5 hours, ~95 km).

Visit:
* Valara and Cheeyappara waterfalls on the way.
* Explore local markets or take a leisurely walk through tea gardens.

Stay: Munnar

Day 3
Munnar Sightseeing

Visit:
* Eravikulam National Park: Home to the Nilgiri Tahr.
* Mattupetty Dam* and *Echo Point*
* Kundala Lake: Enjoy boating.
* Tea Museum: Learn about the history of tea in the region.

Stay: Munnar

Day 4
Munnar to Cochin

Travel to Cochin: Morning drive (4.5 hours, ~130 km).

Visit:
* Chinese Fishing Nets and Fort Kochi Beach
* St. Francis Church and Jewish Synagogue
* Mattancherry Palace
* Sunset cruise on the backwaters

Stay: Cochin

Day 5
Cochin to Alappuzha (Alleppey)

Travel to Alappuzha: Morning drive (1.5 hours, ~60 km).

Houseboat Cruise: Embark on a houseboat for a day cruise through the backwaters.

Lunch: Traditional Kerala cuisine on the houseboat.

Evening: Disembark and check-in at a hotel.

Stay: Alappuzha

Day 6
Alappuzha to Thiruvananthapuram

Travel to Thiruvananthapuram: Morning drive (4 hours, ~150 km).

Visit:
* Padmanabhaswamy Temple: A famous Hindu temple.
* Napier Museum and Shangumugham Beach

Stay: Thiruvananthapuram

Day 7
Thiruvananthapuram to Poovar

Morning: Visit the Kuthiramalika Palace and Sri Chitra Art Gallery

Travel to Poovar: Late morning drive (1 hour, ~30 km).

Afternoon: Relax at the Poovar Beach and explore the nearby village.

Evening: Enjoy a sunset cruise along the backwaters.

Stay: Poovar

Day 8
Poovar to Kanyakumari

Travel to Kanyakumari: Morning drive (2 hours, ~70 km).

Visit:
* Vivekananda Rock Memorial and Thiruvalluvar Statue
* Kanyakumari Temple
* Sunset Viewpoint

Stay: Kanyakumari

Day 9
Kanyakumari to Madurai

Morning: Witness the sunrise at Kanyakumari.

Travel to Madurai: Morning drive (4 hours, ~250 km).

Visit:
* Meenakshi Amman Temple
* Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace
* Gandhi Memorial Museum

Stay: Madurai

Day 10
Madurai to Trichy

Return to Trichy: Morning drive (2.5 hours, ~135 km).

Arrival in Trichy: Afternoon/evening.

Departure: Drop at Trichy airport/railway station.
