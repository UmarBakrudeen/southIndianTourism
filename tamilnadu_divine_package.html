Tamilnadu Divine Package

The Tamil Nadu Divine Tour Package offers a 7 to 10-day spiritual journey to key temples like Meenakshi Temple in Madurai, Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur, and Rameshwaram. The package includes accommodation, transportation, and guided tours, providing a profound spiritual experience and insights into Tamil Naduâ€™s rich cultural and architectural heritage.

Package Detail

No. of Days: 10 Days
Day 1
Trichy

Arrival in Trichy: Morning

Visit:
* Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, Srirangam
* Jambukeswarar Temple, Thiruvanaikaval
* Uchi Pillayar Temple, Rockfort

Stay: Trichy

Day 2
Trichy to Thanjavur

Travel to Thanjavur: Early morning drive (1 hour)

Visit:
* Brihadeeswarar Temple
* Thanjavur Palace

Afternoon: Drive to Kumbakonam (1.5 hours)

Visit:
* Adi Kumbeswarar Temple
* Sarangapani Temple

Stay: Kumbakonam

Day 3
Kumbakonam to Rameshwaram

Travel to Rameshwaram: Early morning drive (6 hours)

Visit:
* Ramanathaswamy Temple
* Dhanushkodi

Evening: Explore Rameshwaram town.

Stay: Rameshwaram

Day 4
Rameshwaram to Thiruchendur

Travel to Thiruchendur: Morning drive (3 hours)

Visit:
* Thiruchendur Murugan Temple

Afternoon: Explore the local area.

Stay: Thiruchendur

Day 5
Thiruchendur to Madurai

Travel to Madurai: Morning drive (3 hours)

Visit:
* Meenakshi Amman Temple
* Thiruparankundram Murugan Temple

Evening: Explore local markets.

Stay: Madurai

Day 6
Madurai to Palani

Travel to Palani: Early morning drive (2.5 hours)

Visit:
* Palani Murugan Temple

Afternoon: Explore Palani town.

Stay: Palani

Day 7
Palani to Tiruvannamalai

Travel to Tiruvannamalai: Early morning drive (5 hours)

Visit:
* Arunachaleswarar Temple

Afternoon: Explore Skandashram and Virupaksha Cave.

Stay: Tiruvannamalai

Day 8
Tiruvannamalai to Kanchipuram

Travel to Kanchipuram: Morning drive (2 hours)

Visit:
* Kamakshi Amman Temple
* Ekambareswarar Temple

Evening: Explore local silk shops.

Stay: Kanchipuram

Day 9
Kanchipuram to Trichy

Return to Trichy: Morning drive (5.5 hours)

Visit (if time permits):
* Samayapuram Mariamman Temple

Stay: Trichy

Day 10
Departure from Trichy

Free time: Last-minute shopping or sightseeing.

Evening: Drop at Trichy airport/railway station for departure.
