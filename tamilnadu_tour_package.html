Tamilnadu: Land of Temples

Tamil Nadu, in southern India, is a culturally rich state known for its ancient temples. It features diverse landscapes, from the hill stations of Ooty to the beaches of Chennai and Kanyakumari. Renowned for its classical arts, vibrant festivals, and flavorful cuisine, Tamil Nadu offers a unique blend of tradition and natural beauty.

Package Detail

No. of Days: 10 Days
Day 1
Trichy

Arrival: Check-in at your hotel in Trichy.

Visit:
* Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple: A major pilgrimage site and architectural marvel.
* Rockfort Temple: Known for its ancient architecture and panoramic city views.
* Jambukeswarar Temple: Dedicated to Lord Shiva, representing the water element.

Stay: Trichy

Day 2
Trichy to Tanjore

Travel to Tanjore: Morning drive (1.5 hours, ~60 km).

Visit:
* Brihadeeswarar Temple: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, renowned for its grand architecture.
* Thanjavur Palace and Art Gallery: Home to ancient artifacts and Tanjore paintings.
* Saraswathi Mahal Library: A historic library with a rare collection of manuscripts.

Stay: Tanjore

Day 3
Tanjore to Kumbakonam

Travel to Kumbakonam: Morning drive (1 hour, ~40 km).

Visit:
* Adi Kumbeswarar Temple: A prominent Shiva temple in Kumbakonam.
* Sarangapani Temple: Dedicated to Lord Vishnu.
* Swamimalai Murugan Temple: One of the Arupadai Veedu temples dedicated to Lord Murugan.

Stay: Kumbakonam

Day 4
Kumbakonam to Rameshwaram

Travel to Rameshwaram: Morning drive (6 hours, ~240 km).

Visit:
* Ramanathaswamy Temple: Famous for its long corridors and sacred water tanks.
* Pamban Bridge: India’s first sea bridge with stunning views.
* Dhanushkodi: Explore the ruins and the beautiful beach.

Stay: Rameshwaram

Day 5
Rameshwaram to Tiruchendur

Travel to Tiruchendur: Morning drive (4 hours, ~230 km).

Visit:
* Tiruchendur Murugan Temple: One of the Arupadai Veedu, located on the shores of the Bay of Bengal.
* Valli Cave Temple: A small cave temple dedicated to Valli, consort of Lord Murugan.
* Beach: Relax by the Tiruchendur beach.

Stay: Tiruchendur

Day 6
Tiruchendur to Kanyakumari

Travel to Kanyakumari: Morning drive (3 hours, ~130 km).

Visit:
 * Vivekananda Rock Memorial and Thiruvalluvar Statue.
* Kanyakumari Temple: A famous pilgrimage site.
* Sunset Viewpoint: Enjoy the sunset at the southernmost tip of India.

Stay: Kanyakumari

Day 7
Kanyakumari to Madurai

Morning: Witness the sunrise at Kanyakumari.

Travel to Madurai: Morning drive (4 hours, ~245 km).

Visit:
* Meenakshi Amman Temple: A majestic temple complex with stunning sculptures.
* Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace: An architectural blend of Dravidian and Rajput styles.
* Gandhi Memorial Museum: Showcasing the life and works of Mahatma Gandhi.

Stay: Madurai

Day 8
Madurai to Kodaikanal

Travel to Kodaikanal: Morning drive (3 hours, ~115 km).

Visit:
* Coaker’s Walk: A scenic walking path offering breathtaking views.
* Bryant Park: A beautiful botanical garden.
* Kodaikanal Lake: Enjoy boating or cycling around the lake.

Stay: Kodaikanal

Day 9
Kodaikanal Sightseeing

Visit:
* Pillar Rocks: Tall, natural rock pillars with stunning views.
* Guna Caves: Also known as Devil’s Kitchen, a fascinating spot.
* Silver Cascade Falls: A picturesque waterfall.

Stay: Kodaikanal

Day 10
 Kodaikanal to Trichy

Return to Trichy: Morning drive (4.5 hours, ~200 km).

Arrival in Trichy: Afternoon/evening.

Departure: Drop at Trichy airport/railway station.
